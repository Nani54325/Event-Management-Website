About Us

We are the platform for conducting the events.

AUMN Events was founded on 2021 to conduct the biggest events in the world and to take care of the users with utmost priority.

People who want to participate in the events or who want their events to be conducted, sign up and user you login to contact us. Our best team will be in contact with you if they think that your event is best to go. You can also register for the events and you can contact us 24/7 if there is any issue.
